◆ RoundMode

enum mpdecpp::RoundMode
strong

The rounding modes taken from mpdecimal. Details look at https://www.bytereef.org/mpdecimal/doc/libmpdec/context.html#rounding

Enumerator
RoundUp 

round away from 0.

RoundDown 

round toward 0 (truncate).

RoundCeiling 

round toward +infinity.

RoundFloor 

round toward -infinity.

RoundHalfUp 

0.5 is rounded up.

RoundHalfDown 

0.5 is rounded down.

RoundHalfEven 

0.5 is rounded to even.

Round05Up 

round zero or five away from 0.

RoundTrunc 

truncate, but set infinities.

Default 

Default rounding mode (on initialization this is RoundHalfEven).
